Adeptus Evangelion - Adnascentia of Ideals

While this particular project is indeed a tabletop RPG, it is definitely of note as being a stellar performance by all members involved. With timid Steiner, choleric Phillip, hopeful Elidia, jovial Takumi, probably-the-protagonist Yuki, and hardened Azami (she's mine btw), it's either crazy antics, or depressive page after page of self-hate and hopelessness. But hey, it's fun.
